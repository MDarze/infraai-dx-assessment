# 8.4 — Score-based email drip sequence

**Priority:** P3 · **Effort:** M · **Theme:** Integrations

## Problem
Assessment-completed leads cool off if not nurtured. A score-band-aware email drip can keep them engaged: low-score leads get a "starter pack," mid-score get a "transformation roadmap," high-score get an "advanced AI" pitch.

## Solution
Once 2.2 (email report) and 8.1 (webhook) exist, this is mostly orchestration. Use Resend / Mailchimp / customer.io to schedule a 3-email sequence triggered on assessment completion:

- Day 0: report email (already sent via 2.2)
- Day 3: case study or content piece relevant to their score band
- Day 7: direct CTA — "want to discuss your results?"

Score band determined at trigger time and used to select the email template variant.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Drip triggered by webhook (8.1)
- [ ] Three score bands: 0–40 / 41–70 / 71–100
- [ ] Per-band content variants
- [ ] Unsubscribe link in every email (legal requirement)
- [ ] Stop drip if user books a meeting (Calendly webhook → drip stop)

## Files
- External (drip platform)
- Backend: webhook payload includes score band

## Out of scope
- In-app drip configuration
- A/B testing email variants (later)
