# 5.5 — Branded completion confetti

**Priority:** P3 · **Effort:** S · **Theme:** Trust & conversion

## Problem
Clicking Analyze on the last question is a moment of accomplishment for the respondent. The current transition is functional and emotionless. Tiny moment, oversized payoff opportunity.

## Solution
On Analyze click, show a brief celebratory animation before navigating to Results — a few brand-colored particles (canvas confetti or pure CSS), a quick success toast like "Generating your report…", then the Results render.

Keep the animation short (<1.5s) so power users don't feel slowed down. Skip entirely on `prefers-reduced-motion`.

Use a tiny library like `canvas-confetti` (~3KB) or hand-roll with CSS keyframes.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Animation plays on Analyze click
- [ ] Total duration <1.5s
- [ ] Skipped under prefers-reduced-motion
- [ ] Brand colors only (no rainbow)
- [ ] Doesn't block rendering of Results

## Files
- `src/App.tsx` — fire animation in `finishSurvey`
- `src/ui/Confetti.tsx` (new) or canvas-confetti dep

## Out of scope
- Per-section celebrations (overkill)
- Score-based animation (e.g., bigger celebration for higher score)
