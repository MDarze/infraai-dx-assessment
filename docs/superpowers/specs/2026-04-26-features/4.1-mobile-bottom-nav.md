# 4.1 — Mobile bottom nav for Survey

**Priority:** P1 · **Effort:** S · **Theme:** Mobile

## Problem
The Survey's three buttons (Back, Prev, Next/Analyze) sit in a flex row mid-content. On phones the row is cramped, the buttons are small, and they're not in the thumb's reach zone. Field engineers using PWA on phones can't progress confidently.

## Solution
On viewports `<768px`, render the action buttons as a fixed bottom bar (`fixed bottom-0 left-0 right-0`) with a top hairline border and a backdrop blur on `bg-bg/95`. Increase button height to 48px (touch target). Page content gets `pb-20` to avoid being hidden behind the bar.

On desktop, keep the inline button row as today.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Buttons are a fixed bottom bar at <768px
- [ ] Buttons are inline at ≥768px
- [ ] No content hidden behind the bar (pb on the wrapper)
- [ ] Bottom bar respects safe-area-inset-bottom on iOS
- [ ] Renders correctly in RTL and LTR
