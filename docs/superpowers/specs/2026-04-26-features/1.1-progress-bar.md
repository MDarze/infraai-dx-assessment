# 1.1 — Survey progress bar

**Priority:** P1 · **Effort:** S · **Theme:** Survey completion

## Problem
The "question 7/24" counter is too quiet. Users without a continuous progress signal abandon long surveys. We have no telemetry to confirm but it's a known funnel killer in form research.

## Solution
A 2px-tall horizontal bar at the top of the Survey wrapper (above the role-section card), filled `bg-brand` to width `(idx + 1) / questions.length × 100%`. Animate width transition over 200ms. Background of the unfilled portion is `bg-surface-alt`. RTL-safe (fill from logical start, not physical left).

For multi-respondent assessments, optionally show a faint secondary bar showing combined progress across all respondents.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Bar appears at the top of the Survey page on every question
- [ ] Width updates smoothly when navigating Next/Prev
- [ ] Visible at desktop, tablet, and phone widths
- [ ] Renders correctly in both RTL (AR) and LTR (EN)
- [ ] No layout shift when it animates

## Files
- `src/pages/Survey.tsx` — add the bar element above the existing content
- No new primitives needed; can be inline `<div>` or wrap in a small `Progress` component

## Out of scope
- Per-respondent breakdown (covered by 1.4)
- Animated transitions between questions (covered by 3.7)
