# 6.5 — Client portal (read-only)

**Priority:** P3 · **Effort:** L · **Theme:** Backoffice

## Problem
Once you've assessed a client, they have no way to revisit their report without contacting you. They lose the artifact, you become a bottleneck, and there's no ongoing relationship surface.

## Solution
A separate authenticated frontend (or new route in the existing app) where each client can:
- See their past assessments
- Re-download report PDFs
- See progress against recommendations they marked as done
- Schedule a new assessment

Authentication: magic-link emails (no passwords). Each magic link tied to a contactEmail on Company.

This is significant scope; consider only when you have 20+ clients and they're asking.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Magic-link auth flow
- [ ] Client sees only their own assessments
- [ ] Read-only, no edit
- [ ] Mobile-responsive

## Files
- New frontend (or major addition to existing one)
- Backend: auth, magic-link mailer, scoped queries

## Out of scope
- Client comments / collaboration
- Client editing of their data (would conflict with assessor workflow)
